Our Story

More Than a Haircut.

Northline Barbers opened its doors in 2014 with a simple belief: every man deserves a great barber. Since then, we've built a reputation for precision, consistency, and a shop that feels like yours.

  • Master barbers with 5+ years of professional training

  • Walk-ins welcome; appointments always prioritised
